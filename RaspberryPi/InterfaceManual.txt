Enabling/disabling interfaces in Raspberry pi:

Using monitor or vnc viewer:
1. Go to the raspberry symbol
2. Navigate to 'Preferences' and on to 'Raspberry PI Configuration'
3. In the new window, navigate to 'Interfaces'
4. Enable or disable the interfaces as you wish and confirm with 'OK'

Using ssh:

1. sudo raspi-config
2. in new window navigate to Interfacing Options
3. enable or disable the interfaces as you wish and confirm with 'finish'
